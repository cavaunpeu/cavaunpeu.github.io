About Me

I'm Will, a technologist interested in ML research and building reliable, real-world systems. Throughout my career, I've spanned the full RE ↔ MLE ↔ SWE gammut, from developing novel NLP algorithms for global enterprise customers at ASAPP Research, to building on-chain asset management platforms with $100M+ under management at Gauntlet. I enjoy working on ambiguous, high-leverage problems with talented peers.

My technical interests include words like reinforcement learning, game theory, simulation, the quantification of risk and uncertainty, and LLMs. Throughout this blog, you'll find ample writing on these topics.

I also research sequential decision-making problems in imperfect information games. You can play against my models at monopolydeal.ai. If you are a researcher or practictioner in this space, I'd love to connect.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, biking, and learning foreign languages (Spanish, French, and Russian). I once rode a bicycle 7,500 kilometers from Turkey to Kyrgyzstan through the Caucasus and Central Asian "Stans."
